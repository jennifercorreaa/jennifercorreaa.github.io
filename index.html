Why Most People Admire El Chapo

Joaquin Guzman also known as "El Chapo" (which is mexican slang for shorty) is the most known Mexican drug lord and founded the Sinaloa cartel in 1989, and over time building it into an immensely profitable global drug-trafficking operation. While as other people love him or admire him because of the many times he's escaped top-security prisons and just police in general. Others also admire him for his hate towards the president and love for Mexico's culture and drugs.

El Chapo's Timeline:

  • April 4, 1957: Guzman was born in the Sinaloa state.
  • 1970's - 1980's: He rises through the ranks of the powerful Gauadalajara Cartel led by Miguel Angel Felix Gallardo.
  • June 10, 1992: El Chapo's first capture in Guatemala.
  • 1995: Guzman is convicted and sentenced to 20 years in prison.
  • Jan 19, 2001: Guzman escapes from top-security prison with bribing guards.
  • Feb 22, 2014: Guzman is captured in Mazatlan after hiding in tunnels for days.
  • July 11, 2015: Guzman escapes through a mile-long tunnel from Mexico's top-security prison.
  • Jan 8, 2016: Is re-captured in Los Mochis,Sinaloa after a shootout with Mexican marines.
